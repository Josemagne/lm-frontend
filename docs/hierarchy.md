# Hierarchy

### Subject

- Subject
  - BookCollection
    - Book
      - Chapter
        - SubChapter
  - ArticleCollection
    - Article
      - Chapter
        - SubChapter

---

### Data that can have notes:

- Book
- Article
- Chapter
- SubChapter
- LoanWord

### Words

- LoanWord: A word that is unknown to somebody.
- GlossaryWord: A word that strictly belongs to the Subject
- KeyWord: A word that is strongly associated to the data at hand.
